Beyond Eternity: A prelude of Scripted Faiths

Beyond Eternity is a visual novel game aimed to ignore the 4th wall and immerse the player in a reality-bending story about heartbreak and betrayal.
The game's story has you trying out a new application known as "Border Box" which advertises itself as an infinite cloud storage system.
However, after entering the program, your UI is immediately attacked by a mysterious being that resided within Borderboxâ€” or rather, Borderbox brought you to the being.

Eventually, the attack is stopped by what seems to be the being's older sister, who asks a favor from you. Your goal within Borderbox is to look for
the eldest sister and help her with what she needs. You're not told what she needs help with, just that she needs help.
After pairing your soul with a body, you're sent off into the world Borderbox connected you to: The Null Crossing.

Moments would pass and you even meet the eldest sister, Khueen. Here, she reveals what she needs help with: To get rid of you.
Specifically, the body your soul was paired to is the body of Khueen's lover.
After this reveal, you're now tasked to converse with Blazhe, the second-eldest sister who paired your soul with the body.
Whenever you start Beyond Eternity's .exe file, you're asked what your name is.
According to Khueen, if you use this opportunity to ask Blazhe to
'Please separate my soul' she would oblige and give Freedom control over his body once more.
How the story ends would be left for the game to tell.

Most of the game was developed by me, with the non-pixel art created by a small group of artist friends and voice acting done by voice actors.

Features the game include:

  • 4~ hours of story/gameplay
  • 1.5k lines of dialogue
  • 50+ Randomized dialogue that makes everyone's play-through different.
  • 6 FUN puzzle minigames
  • Original Soundtrack with 20+ tracks
  • 5 Unique Endings

Just Another Day: 2020

Created as a group project for my first semester finals output in the 11th Grade, I got help from an artist and level designer in my team to create Just Another Day.
The game is meant to help deliver information during the Covid Pandemic on how to avoid getting infected.
Your task in the game is to complete errands while avoiding infection. You may stay away from groups of people to avoid infection.

This game was also my first time utilizing and learning how to work with the Godot game engine.

Features the game include:
  • 2 fully-playable levels
  • Randomized Errands
  • Original Soundtrack with 3+ tracks
  • Fun Speedrunning potential
